A Himalayan Retreat

Kartik Villa is located inside the serene Queen Meadows Resort in Ranikhet, Uttarakhand. Accessible via a scenic 600-metre hike through tall pine trees, the pathway is well-laid with comfortable resting points along the way. The hike is slightly challenging and may not be suitable for elderly guests.

Experience the peaceful energy of Devbhoomi (Land of Gods), surrounded by pristine pine forests and breathtaking valley views. Perfect for work-from-mountains, family vacations, and slow-living retreats.
